# Demo script — Agentic Inbox

Narrated screen recording, 2–3 minutes. Walk a **new** user through the inbox the way they'd meet it: an inbox the agent already cleared while they were out. Timed beat sheet; one line of suggested narration per beat, natural spoken register. Beats marked **[IF BUILT]** cover the chat sidepanel — skip them cleanly if that scope was cut and the script still lands.

> Setup before recording: triage has already run, results persisted. Land on `/` with the processed inbox. Mic check — you need to be audible.

---

**0:00–0:20 — Land on the processed inbox (the digest)**
Open on `/`, digest summary block visible at the top of the list.
> "This is an inbox for a construction project manager who's been out a few days. While they were away, an agent worked the backlog — 80 emails. Right up top: what it did. This many handled for you, this many need your attention, this many filed away."

**0:20–0:45 — What the agent did for you (auto-handled)**
Scroll to a Done-for-you item; open it to show the rationale + key facts.
> "The routine stuff it just handled. Here's an RFI — it drafted a reply, sent it, and left a one-line note on why. I can open any of these and read the full reasoning and the facts it pulled out. I don't have to; that's the point. But it's here if I want it."

**0:45–1:05 — Undo one (recovery is one click)**
On that auto-handled item, hit Undo; show the toast and the item dropping back to Needs Attention.
> "And if it got one wrong — one click. Undo. The send is retracted, the email comes back to my pile, and the undo itself is logged. Nothing's ever stuck."

**1:05–1:35 — What it refused to do, and why (open a sensitive item)**
Scroll to the pinned approvals at the top. Open a sensitive one — a change order or a claim. Read the rationale aloud.
> "Now the part that matters. These are pinned at the top — the agent would not touch them. This is a change order, real money on the line. It drafted a reply, but it stopped and it's asking me. Here's exactly why it flagged it, in plain language. It never sends anything that could cost money or create legal exposure."

**1:35–2:00 — Approve one, deny one**
On one approval, click Approve (show it moving to Done). On another, click Deny (show it staying with the human / filed for manual handling).
> "So I decide. This draft looks right — approve, and now it sends. This one I'll handle myself — deny, and it stays with me, no draft goes out. Two clicks, and I've cleared the sensitive queue."

**2:00–2:20 — [IF BUILT] Chat sidepanel for the odd follow-up**
Open the chat panel; type something like "undo the reply to Rachel" or "what's still waiting on me". Show the tool-call badge and the inbox updating.
> "For anything off the beaten path, there's a sidepanel. 'What's still waiting on me?' — same agent, same tools, and whatever it does shows up right in the list."

**2:20–2:40 — Close on the trace / audit trail**
Open the sidebar Agent Trace. Scroll the timeline of entries.
> "And everything — every auto-send, every approval, every undo, whether the agent did it or I did — lands here in one trace. That's the whole story of what happened to this inbox, top to bottom."

**2:40–3:00 — Wrap**
Back to the digest at the top.
> "So: the agent clears the routine, stops on anything sensitive, tells me why in plain language, and every call it makes is one click to reverse. Five minutes between meetings and the inbox is handled."

---

### If chat was cut

Drop the **2:00–2:20** beat. Give 1:35–2:00 (approve/deny) a little more room, then go straight to the trace at 2:20. The script still comes in under three minutes and every hard requirement — auto-handling, sensitive deferral with rationale, human approve/deny, undo, audit trail — is on screen.

### Beat checklist (hit all of these on camera)

- [ ] Digest summary (counts) on landing
- [ ] Open an auto-handled item → rationale + key facts
- [ ] Undo an auto-action → toast + back to Needs Attention
- [ ] Open a sensitive item → read why it was deferred
- [ ] Approve one → it sends
- [ ] Deny one → stays with the human
- [ ] [IF BUILT] one chat command that changes the inbox
- [ ] Agent Trace timeline
